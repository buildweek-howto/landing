Learn

How-To started with a simple mission: make your life better, one how-to at a time. How-To has everything you need for a healthier, happier, and more productive day. Whether you’re a consumer or a creator, it’s never been easier to find, create and share tips and tricks with like-minded lifehackers.

We know resolutions can be hard to keep, and if self-help books worked they’d put themselves out of business. How-to wants you to have a better life without all that pressure to live better. Just practical, helpful how-tos that you can use.

Discover

From the mundane to the magical, How-To lets you browse, search and filter how-tos to suit your needs. With our easy to use tools, How-To will take you from IDK to DIY in no time flat. No matter what you're looking for, you’ll always leave here a little wiser than you came.

Contribute

Fancy yourself a how-to guru? How-To makes it easy to create and edit your own how-tos and share them with our community of passionate learners. Show off your ingenuity and help us simplify life for everyone.
